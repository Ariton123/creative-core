# Creative Core

Creative Core is a Digital Art & Game Development Association based in Ohrid, North Macedonia.

The association was founded in May 2026 with the goal of supporting creative collaboration, technical learning, and community development around digital art, game development, and interactive media.

---

## Purpose

Creative Core aims to bring together people interested in digital creativity, game development, art, design, and technology.

The long-term goal is to create a space where members can share ideas, build projects, learn from each other, and develop creative and technical skills together.

---

## Areas of Interest

- Game development
- Digital art
- Interactive media
- Creative technology
- Workshops and learning sessions
- Community events
- Project brainstorming and prototyping
- Mentorship and skill development
