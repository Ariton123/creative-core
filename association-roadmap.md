# Creative Core Roadmap

Creative Core was founded in May 2026 as a Digital Art & Game Development Association based in Ohrid, North Macedonia.

## Initial Goals

- Establish a strong online presence
- Create a community of artists, developers, and creators
- Organize workshops and educational sessions
- Support project collaboration and prototyping
- Promote game development and digital creativity
- Connect students and young professionals with industry opportunities

## Long-Term Vision

Creative Core aims to become a sustainable creative and technical community that encourages learning, experimentation, collaboration, and innovation across digital art and game development.
